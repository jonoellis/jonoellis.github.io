Way back in time I brewed a stout. The first of some, not of many. It would be nice to own a brewery and, at that time at least, I thought "Mouthfull Of Hops Brewery" worked. That was until I realised that brewing at scale is more laborious than I have time for and I've not got the drive to do it frequently enough to be more than okay or to build a company in a very compeititive field. The beer was "Fifty Shades Of Stout #1 - Treacle & Chilli". It was yum but imperfect. The "fifty shades" thing is a bit passé now but the idea of it was that there are a lot of flavours that work with stout and why wouldn't the series just work through a bunch based on available ingredients and whimsey? Stout's my favourite beer style anyway. That in turn got me thinking that maybe every flavour of stout has already been created and shouldn't that be documented?... so I made a list of flavours and set off to research them.
