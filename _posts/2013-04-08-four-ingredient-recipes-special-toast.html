Norwegias have a word, pålegg, that means anything that you put in a sandwich. Is marzipan pålegg? Yes, yes it is; marzipan can go on anything!

To make this delicious sandwich:

  • Toast
  • Butter
  • Apricot jam
  • Marzipan

Yum.
