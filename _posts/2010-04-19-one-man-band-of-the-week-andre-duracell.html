Cheers to Mr Neilson for the tip off about this guy - a seriously good find! He's called Andre Duracell and he plays drums. He's a one man band but not in the conventional sense - he uses a full drum kit to trigger audio samples/loops and the sound that he creates - ALL ON HIS OWN - is almost impossible to believe. He's playing in Edinburgh on the 25th of May so I'm hoping to see him then.
