Type the following into Google:

-inurl:(htm|html|php) intitle:"index of" +"last modified" +"parent directory" +description +size +(mp3) "BAND NAME"

Try that search by clicking here

This search lets you find mp3's that people are openly sharing. There may be some legal issues with this and I don't condone using it - but this does show you how powerful Google is!
