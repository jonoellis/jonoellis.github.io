Bear In Heaven are one of those bands that I didn't think that I'd like very much when I first heard them but then I heard "Lovesick Teenagers" and "Wholehearted Mess". Bits remind me of the Scissor Sisters, other bits remind me of The Killers. They sound a bit '80s (but in a good way). Go listen and tell me what you think!
