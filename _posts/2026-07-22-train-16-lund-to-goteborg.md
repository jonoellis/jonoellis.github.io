Drizzle. Spacious and chilled in a fast train. Self-serve coffee and chocolate. Mostly countryside, a little coast. Fields filled with geese, fields filled with crows, then also fields filled with the usual stuff like cows and crops. Rich, arable lands. Also just rich. The final announcement wished us a pleasant continued day. So we went to their lounge and ate their crackers and soft cheese. The day continues to be pleasant.
