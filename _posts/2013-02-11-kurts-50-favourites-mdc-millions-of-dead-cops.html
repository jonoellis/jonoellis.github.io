Angry... MDC's "Millions of Dead Cops" is definitely an angry album. Hardcore punk of the old-school variety. Way more angry than Nirvana. Not as tuneful as it could be but it has some awesome lyrical moments, "John Wayne Was a Nazi" being one of the standout moments!
