Welcome to my [Rolodex](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Rolodex) - inspired by [kottke.org](http://kottke.org). 

Here are a generous handful sites I think are worth dipping into regularly:
1. [Minute Cryptic - daily puzzle](http://minutecryptic.com)
2. [The Edinburgh Minute - daily Edinburgh news(letter) from Michael MacLeod ]([http://edinburghminute.substack.com](https://www.edinburghminute.com/)) 
3. [kottke - the home of fine hypertext products since 1998](http://kottke.org) 
4. [Hackaday - geeks geeking in the best way](https://hackaday.com/)
5. [things magazine - infrequent but always inspiring](https://www.thingsmagazine.net/)
6. [Cory Doctorow's Pluralistic](https://pluralistic.net/)
7. [swissmiss - design inspiration](https://www.swiss-miss.com/)
8. [Laughing Squid - weird internet](http://laughingsquid.com)
9. [Radio Revolt - internet radio stations of the world](http://radiorevolt.no)
10. [mnmlist - some guidance on enoughness](http://mnmlist.com)
11. [Storythings - behind the curtain of writing and media](https://storythings.com/newsletters/)
