--- layout: post title: Dirty Pirate Popsicles author: Jono published: true date: 2013-09-25T21:39:07+0000 categories: - cooking ---


In a pan, warm and disolve:

  • Coffee powder
  • Brown sugar
  • Vanilla extract
  • Water

Cool then mix with:

  • Coke (with the fizz shaken out)
  • Southern Comfort
  • Vodka

Freeze and enjoy.
