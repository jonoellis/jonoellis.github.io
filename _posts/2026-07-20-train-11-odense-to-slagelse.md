Bus after bus after bus of people arriving to get on to a full train. First class sardines. Logistics make no sense: bus train bus train when we'd have got there faster by bus the busses we were on. We're in good company, Dane and Deutsche confused and cross and tired also. Passing sea with choppy waves. Land-bridge becomes tunnel. Who knows what time even is now.
