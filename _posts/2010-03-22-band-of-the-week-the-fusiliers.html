Leith based "The Fusiliers" have a cool sound that initially made me think of "Tourists In The Warzone" (except with a more Edinburghy accent) and they're definitely worth a listen. "She Cries" is a brilliant track but if you only stay on their MySpace for a couple of minutes you've got to check out "The Way" first. Browse their Facebook for more of the banter.
