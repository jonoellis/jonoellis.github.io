In honour of my winter beard this week's Band Of The Week are The Beards. Hailing from Australia they do comedy songs mostly about beards. I was going to pick "It Only Takes A Fortnight..." but I thought that "If Your Dad Doesn't Have a Beard, You've Got Two Mums" had too ridiculous a title to ignore. Enjoy!
