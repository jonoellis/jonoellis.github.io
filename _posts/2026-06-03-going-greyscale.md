Tip: turn your devices greyscale, it makes them way less appealing. I've been spending more time in greyscale mode and less time on my phone as a result. Love it.

On Android, you can add a quick toggle using the volume keys. To do this it's Colour Correction > Shortcut > Volume keys and on the screen before toggle to Greyscale then all you need to do is press and hold both the volume up and volume down buttons for a couple of seconds to get you in/out of greyscale. Handy.
