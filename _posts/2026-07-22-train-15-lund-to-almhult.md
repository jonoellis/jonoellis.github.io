Sat in the wrong spot on a train that splits. We moved. Local train zooming past corn fields, red brick buildings, forest and windmills. A group of older teenage German Scouts with massive bags head off to the woods. Can't see the forest for the trees. Lakes with pontoons for swimming. Deer in a glade. Many wee houses dotted around. Train was indeed split in two, we were not. Lots of small towns - one with a miniature wild west in the town centre (Osby). Sunshiny. Welcome to IKEAland.
