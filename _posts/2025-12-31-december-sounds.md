The year definitely went out with a hectic bang! Some great stuff including double work Christmas nights out (so much lovely food and perfect company - I work with some awesome people), Edinburgh schools Christmas concert killing it, some volunteering at the school concert, a great trip to the Borders, hosting a jolly Christmas crew, a trip North, a blue cheese December, Stereolab... some middle stuff including the wonderful The Fifth Elephant play but it was canned 2/3rds of the way through due to an incident.. and then there was the other stuff from the trival but personal car woes to the global and horrific topics of Epstein, Ukraine, the climate, etc. Wow 2026, just wow.
