The busy was exceptional: exam revision into exams, Tri Camp so we went to Troon, NYOS so we went to the #cabin and I lined the shed and built a workbench and walked round Redpoints, lovely dinners and socialising, friends up so we went to Inchcolm (loved it), another Band+Biscuits family music-making day, Arkham, school drama shows, tree felling and chopping with my dad, a 20 mile charity walk with the kid, and Glasgow and Edinburgh office days in one week!


* [Heresy - Nine Inch Noise](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=C3cLOE6Phms)

	 It doesn't matter if it's just the same songs being played over and over in new ways, NIN are hitting harder than ever before with this album and I am here for it!


* [White Noise - Kerala Dust](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=vnhGT_G_riE)

	 Rock rave techno baroque electronica? Whatever it is, it's drugs. 


* [Squish - Gretel](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=AlHhdX7xuRE)

	 Popped up and out on 6 Music. One of a very small number of songs ever written to feature the word "squish", yet the second to come out in 2026 (behind pupeteer comedian rocker [Sophie Truax](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=3Y2mc14Yomg)).


* [Hard To Get - Bec Lauder](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Ok3sQYAWPh8)
