I've been waiting for a while to have Come On Gang as my band of the week... but the wait is over - Come On Gang's first single is out now!! Fortune Favours The Brave is so catchy you'll be humming it at bus stops and the video is awesome too - can you name any other band with a tiger fighting a dinosaur in their first music video? No, I didn't think so. You can even get a free copy if you sign up to their mailing list. Enjoy. :)
