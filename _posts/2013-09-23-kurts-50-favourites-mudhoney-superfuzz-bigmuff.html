I love the first track "Need". The way that "In 'n' Out of Grace" opens with the same Wild Angels quote that Primal Scream use in "Loaded" 2 years later amuses me, I just took it as a Primal Scream original! Altogether worthy of a listen to the godfathers of the Seattle grunge scene.
