How wonderful - K67 Berlin are restoring the awesome modular “K67” soviet era plastic street kiosks! Designed by Slovenian architect Saša J. Mächtig, the small buildings were designed to be “agglomerations” that could be joined together in myriad ways.

There’s so much info out there - I recommend checking out this site for the the K67’s plans and history, and this site for some cool kiosk photos.
